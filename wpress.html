Workshop on WORDPRESS

Web Development is a rapidly developing field today and offers vast oppurtunities to anyone interested. Confused with all the technologies and programming languages? The workshop offers training on Worpress, the most popular Content Management System used online which lets you create a website without writing a single line of code.

About WordPress

WordPress is a free and open-source content management system (CMS) based on PHP and MySQL.Features include a plugin architecture and a template system. WordPress was used by more than 23.3% of the top 10 million websites as of January 2015.WordPress is the most popular blogging system in use on the Web,at more than 60 million websites.
